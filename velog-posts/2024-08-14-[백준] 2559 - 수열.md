[[백준] 2559 - 수열](https://www.acmicpc.net/problem/2559)

### 💡 내 코드
```python
n, k = map(int, input().split())
tempList = list(map(int, input().split()))

sumList = sum(tempList[:k])
answer = sumList

s, e = 0, k

for i in range(k, n):
    sumList = sumList - tempList[s] + tempList[e]
    answer = max(answer, sumList)
    s += 1
    e += 1

print(answer)
```
